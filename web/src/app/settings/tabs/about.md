# 🦌 [About Omega Intelligence](https://www.omegaintelligence.ai/)

> **Meet the neweset member of your Deal Team*

**Omega Intelligence** is an AI-powered research and diligence automation platform designed specifically for private-market investors. Our goal is to accelerate deal evaluation by turning fragmented documents—SEC filings, financial models, industry reports—into a unified, searchable knowledge base. Analysts gain rapid access to comparable deals, key metrics and risk indicators, reducing manual work and enabling more confident, data-driven investment decisions.

Upcoming enhancements will include expanded data ingestion capabilities—bringing in more document types and third-party datasets—to enrich our research universe; smarter analytics modules for automated benchmarking, KPI extraction, and risk-flagging; collaborative workspaces with in-platform commenting and review workflows; seamless integration with popular communication tools; and a mobile experience to keep deal teams connected and informed wherever they are.
