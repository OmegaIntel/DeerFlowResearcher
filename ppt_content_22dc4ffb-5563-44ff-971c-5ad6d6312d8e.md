# PE-Backed Healthcare Exits (2020-2025)

---

## The Challenge

*   Identifying PE-backed healthcare exits with high IRR (2020-2025)
*   Focus on deals with ~3-year holding periods
*   Data required: IRR, holding period, subsector, PE firms

---

## Research Approach

*   Web search for news, reports, and databases
*   Target: Healthcare exits and PE activity
*   Limitations: Publicly available IRR data is scarce

---

## General Trends in Healthcare PE

*   Healthcare sector remains highly attractive
*   Strong PE activity in healthcare acquisitions & exits (2020-2025)
*   Specific subsectors of high interest:
    *   Pharma services
    *   Dental
    *   Behavioral health

---

## Data Collection Difficulties

*   Precise IRR figures are typically proprietary
*   Holding periods often not disclosed publicly
*   General web searches are insufficient for detailed deal data
*   Requires specialized financial databases or private reports

---

## Conclusion

*   General PE trends in healthcare are identifiable
*   Obtaining specific IRR and holding period data is challenging
*   Access to specialized financial data is necessary for completion
